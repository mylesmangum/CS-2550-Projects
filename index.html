My Personal Interests

Something that I enjoy doing is to go skiing. I grew up on the East Coast, where there isn't as many large mountains or places to do things like ski or snowboard, but I moved to Utah when I was 17 and was able to discover that year how much I enjoy it. I actually worked at the ski resort Deer Valley for a couple of years before I started school up to be able to get a season pass for free. However, working as a lift operator doesn't pay as well as something where you need a college degree so here I am now!

I also really enjoy puzzles like Rubik's Cubes and Rotating Clocks. I was more into it when I was a kid and had a lot more free time, but I got pretty good at them and have kept the skill. My record is about 7 seconds when I was 14 years old and I have never been lucky enough to beat that record. Now, I average about 25 seconds per solve on a cube and I have a collection of over 15 different puzzles of various shapes and sizes.

Something that relates more to computers is I really enjoy building them. I have been able to build 7 or 8 at this point for myself, friends, and family. However, this is quite the expensive hobby so I just enjoy doing it when I can and hope that someday that can be beneficial to my career or I can afford to build a beast of a setup.
